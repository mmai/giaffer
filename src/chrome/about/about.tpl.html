About

In the fairy tale The Three Princes of Serendip, the king Giaffer sends his three sons to explore the world, hoping they gain wisdom. Inspired by this story, Horace Walpole coined the concept of serendipity

Giaffer's goal is to facilitate the discovery of new Web pages by combining search terms related to subjects randomly selected from a list of pre-defined interests.

It is a web implementation of Integrate your Interests, a hack by Ron Hale Evans from his book Mindhacker.

This extension is built and maintained by Henri Bourcereau. You can send bugs & feature requests on Github.
