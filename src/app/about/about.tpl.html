About

The idea behind Giaffer was proposed by Ron Hale Evans in the chapter Integrate your Interests of his book Mindhacker.

With this hack you can take common ideas and fuse them using 'interest integrator' software to generate extraordinary, unpredictable results.

Giaffer, the king in the fairy tale The Three Princes of Serendip, sent his three sons explore the world. The story inspired Horace Walpole the concept of 'serendipity'.
