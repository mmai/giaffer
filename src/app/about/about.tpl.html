About

In the story The Three Princes of Serendip, from which emerged the concept of serendipity, Giaffer is the king of Serendip who sent his three sons explore the world.

Giaffer is based on an idea by Ron Hale Evans in the chapter Integrate your Interests of his book Mindhacker. Its goal is to facilitate the discovery of new web pages by combining search terms related to topics randomly selected from a list of pre-defined interests. "With this hack you can take common ideas and fuse them using 'interest integrator' software to generate extraordinary, unpredictable results."

Usage

You first need to establish your own list of interesting topics: for each topic, set its name and its associated search terms. You will not have to re-enter this information when you return to this page as they are saved in your browser (with no need of account creation).

Go back to the home page to initiate a random selection and start a search.
